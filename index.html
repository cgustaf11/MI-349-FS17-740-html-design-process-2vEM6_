Short Pitch:

Outer State Enterprises is a Space Launch specialist that entered the market in 2017 to bring affordable, reliable launches to everyone.

Long Pitch:

You want to go to space, and we want to take you there. Competition in this market has been lacking for the past several thousand years, and Outer State Enterprises is here to help change that. Aside from providing citzens, corporations, and governments with lift and transportation capabilities, Outer State is also interested in colonizing and exploiting the world outside of Earth. Be a part of Outer State, and invest in the end of the Universe.
